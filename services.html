Free Checkups


Free Health Checkups at JNTUH Healthcare Center
The JNTUH Healthcare Center offers free health checkups for students and residents of the JNTU quarters. This initiative ensures that the JNTUH community has access to essential preventive care without financial burden.

  • Eligibility: Free health checkups are available for all students and residents living in the JNTU quarters.
  • Preventive Care: These checkups help in early detection of potential health issues, promoting timely intervention and better health outcomes.
  • Cost-Effective:By offering free services, the healthcare center alleviates the financial strain associated with medical consultations and tests, making healthcare accessible to everyone in the community.

Medicines

  • Free of Cost*: Medicines and medical services are provided at no charge for all students and staff.
